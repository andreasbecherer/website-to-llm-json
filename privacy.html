Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Skeletonizer ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data within the Skeletonizer browser extension.

2. Data Collection & Processing

Skeletonizer is designed to be **privacy-first**. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Local Operation: All analysis and data extraction happen locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Website Content: The extension analyzes the active tab's structural layout and computed styles to generate a JSON "skeleton" of the page. This only happens when you explicitly trigger the "Scan" action.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Permissions

To function correctly, the extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Used to access the website you are currently viewing to perform the layout analysis.
  • scripting: Used to execute the analysis logic on the page to extract the necessary structural data.

4. Third-Party Services

Skeletonizer does not use any third-party cookies, trackers, or analytics scripts. Your data stays on your machine.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
